The minting process takes place on our website. Just enter the amount of eggs you’d like to mint and press the button below.

1. Using a computer. Download the Metamask plugin and install it into your browser following the link https://metamask.io/.

2. Using a phone. Download the Metamask built-in browser. Then launch the Metamask application, click the 3 lines on the top left menu in the application and select "Browser". It will open a web browser and you will be able to navigate back to our website.

The purchased NFTs will be stored in your Metamask wallet.
